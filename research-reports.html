Research & Reports

Evidence-Based Understanding

Impulse NGO Network has produced some of the most comprehensive research in India on human trafficking, unsafe migration, child labour, and access to justice. Our studies inform policy frameworks, support rescue agencies, and strengthen cross-border collaboration.

Key Reports

  • Access to Justice for Women in Northeast India – A multi-state analysis exploring barriers faced by women in securing legal support.
  • Exploratory Study of Children in Rat-Hole Mining – The first documented evidence of child labour operations in Jaintia Hills.
  • Cross-Border Trafficking Research – Assessing network routes and vulnerabilities across India, Nepal, Bangladesh & Myanmar.
  • INGON Annual Assessment Reports – Yearly breakdowns of rescues, collaboration impact, survivor cases and justice outcomes.

Why Research Matters

The strength of the Impulse Model lies in its grounding in real data, field evidence, and survivor-led learnings. Our research continues to influence national bodies, government committees, and international organisations.
