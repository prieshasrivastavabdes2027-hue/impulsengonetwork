Who We Are

Impulse NGO Network is a pioneering force working across India and South Asia to prevent trafficking, protect vulnerable communities, rescue survivors, and rebuild futures with dignity. Our work is based on the award-winning Impulse Model, a collaborative framework that unites law enforcement, government agencies, NGOs, and communities.

Our Journey

From a grassroots initiative in Meghalaya to an internationally recognized anti-trafficking network, INGON has built 25+ years of systemic change. Our work now impacts India, Nepal, Myanmar, and Bangladesh.

“Every rescue is a beginning. Every survivor deserves a second chance at life.”

Our Vision & Mission

As professionals we will work passionately and relentlessly to reduce human trafficking for exploitation by empowering, engaging, and expanding our network of partners using the 6Ps and 6Rs of the globally acknowledged Impulse Model
