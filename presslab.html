Impulse Model Press Lab

A Storytelling Hub for Social Impact

The Impulse Model Press Lab is INGON's media and documentation unit, dedicated to capturing field realities, survivor stories, community resistance, and policy developments. Through photography, investigative writing and documentary-style reporting, the Press Lab brings visibility to human trafficking and labour exploitation.

Investigative Narratives

Our team collaborates with national and international journalists to produce fact-based narratives, spotlighting trafficking routes, rescue operations, and cross-border repatriation processes. These stories influence policymaking, public perception, and international dialogue.
