Media Coverage of Our Campaign

National Attention on Child Labour

The fight against child labour in Meghalaya’s coal mines gained national momentum when major media outlets highlighted INGON’s findings, survivor rescues, and advocacy efforts. Reports helped uncover the human cost of unsafe mining practices and amplified the need for legal accountability.

Press Mentions

  • The Hindu — 2011–2019
  • Hindustan Times — 2012–2020
  • The Times of India — 2014–2021
  • The Shillong Times — 2010–2022
  • NDTV & CNN-IBN Special Reports — 2013–2018
“Media helped bring visibility to those who had no platform — making the invisible visible.”

Why Media Matters

News coverage creates public awareness, pressures institutions, and ensures that exploitation is not ignored. By partnering with responsible journalists and global outlets, INGON continues to advocate for systemic changes rooted in transparency.
